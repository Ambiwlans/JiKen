Test Your Kanji



JiKen uses statistics from users like you to estimate your Kanji knowledge in as few questions as possible (as few as 10 questions gives a reasonable estimate!). Each test reacts to your answers. Answer more questions to train the program and fine tune your estimate.

Hotkeys: ← Know, → Don't Know, ↓ Flip card

Tip: Treat it seriously, no "Well, I should have known that one!" answers.

Keep in mind that this is still in a very alpha state. Report any bugs you find!
