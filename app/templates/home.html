Test Your Kanji


JiKen is kanji test, but instead of blindly asking you a ton of random kanji, it uses machine learning to ask you only the kanji most relevant to you. After only 10 questions, it will estimate how many you know. Keep going to make the estimate more accurate!

When you are done, the test results will make a study list to help prepare for Kanken or JLPT. You can also bookmark your results to see how you improve as you study Japanese. Or share your results and challenge friends.


Answer 10 to start. Keep going for a more accurate estimate!
